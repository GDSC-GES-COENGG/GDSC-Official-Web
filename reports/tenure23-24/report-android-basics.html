Event Report

Android Basics with Jetpack Compose

  1. Home
  2. Events
  3. Android Basics with Jetpack Compose

Summary of event:

• Event Title : Android Basics with Jetpack Compose 🚀
• Event Duration: 7th and 8th January 2024 ⏳
• Number of participants: 600 🌟
• Speakers: Samradni Salunke, Akshada Warkhede, Kasturi Shirole

The "Android Basics with Compose" event series, held on 7th and 8th January, delved deep into foundational Android development concepts. Attendees explored Kotlin programming, the functionality of Android Studio IDE, and the innovative approach of Jetpack Compose for UI design. The sessions combined theoretical knowledge with practical application, offering a comprehensive understanding of Android development essentials.

The highlight of the event was a live demonstration of Jetpack Compose, showcasing its power in simplifying UI development. Participants gained hands-on experience and practical insights, empowering them with the skills needed to embark on their journey as proficient Android developers. Overall, the event series was a success, providing a conducive learning environment and equipping attendees with valuable knowledge in modern Android app development techniques.


Key Takeaways:

• Understanding of fundamental Android development principles.
• Proficiency in using Kotlin for Android app development.
• Familiarity with Android Studio IDE functionalities.
• Practical knowledge in leveraging Jetpack Compose for building responsive UIs.
