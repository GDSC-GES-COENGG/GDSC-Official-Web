Event Report

Inauguration Session

  1. Home
  2. Events
  3. Inauguration Session

Summary of event:

• We had conducted an Inaugural Info Session on 30th August 2021 on YouTube.
• This event emphasized on the info session about what is GDSC , our mission , vision and target , GDSC-GESCOENGG achievements and educational description of our community members.
• Also, Event gave focus on the empowerment and growth of women in Women Tech Makers.
• Perks of being a GDSC member were discussed.


Benefits:

• Students came know about Google developer student club in detail.
• Students learned about the many programmes and events offered by the GDSC.
• They were introduced to the leaders of the many fields.
• Students who successfully completed all activites are eligible for certification and winning amazing goodies
